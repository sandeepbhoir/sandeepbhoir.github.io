## 📖 User Guide

### Creating a Token

1. Open TokenForge.
2. Click on "Create New Token".
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - Token Name
   - Token Symbol
   - Total Supply
   - Divisibility options

4. Review your details and hit "Create".

### Managing Your Token

After creating your token, you can:

- View token details in the “Manage Tokens” section.
- Adjust settings as needed.
- View blockchain transactions associated with your token.
